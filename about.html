ABOUT GIZMO



Gizmo, founded by Jerome P. Viseral, is a premium mechanical keyboard company known for its high-quality keyboards and accessories. One of their notable products is the Gizmo Engineering GK6, a minimalist keyboard crafted entirely from aluminum for an unrivaled typing experience. The case is meticulously machined from a robust block of billet aluminum, giving it a sleek design and a lustrous silver anodization. The keyboard features individual RGB LEDs for each key and is fully programmable using the QMK firmware™. It’s compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux, making it a versatile choice for keyboard enthusiasts2. If you’re looking for top-notch mechanical keyboards, Gizmo Engineering is definitely worth exploring!
